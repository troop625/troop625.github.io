  • Information

    For the past several years BSA Troop 625 has been beautifying our West Haven neighborhood on holidays by posting an American flag in yards. The display of flags looks impressive and patriotic. The time has come to take orders for the upcoming flag season. The cost is $45 per flag for 9 holidays. The funds collected are provided to the scouts who help setup and take down the flags on holidays and are used to offset the cost of their annual summer camp.

  • How Do We Do It?

    BSA Troop 625 retains ownership of the flag and will store it. The flag is mounted by driving a piece of steel rebar into your lawn and the flag pole placed over it. At the end of the holiday, the flag and rebar will be removed. Weather and extenuating circumstances my require some days to be altered. Click Here! to check if you are in our service area

  • Payment Info

    Please make checks payable to BSA Troop 625. Cash is also accepted. Troop 625 scouts will come to your house on a Tuesday evening in June to collect your payment starting with the second Tuesday.
